<img src="/images/E%20Learning/SLS_Login.jpg" style="width:75%"/>

**A) <u>Logging In</u>**

Click [here](https://vle.learning.moe.edu.sg/login)	to access Student Learning Space

**Username:** *[First 5 letters of student's name] + [last 5 characters of student's BC number]*
<br>**Password:**
<br>Refer to Student Handbook for username and password. Use "forgot password" function on SLS login page to reset password if needed. Remember to update Student Handbook if password was reset.  

**B) <u>Accessing Your Homework</u>**
<br>You can find the groups that you are in under the "User Group" tab. 
To find your homework, go to the "Due Soon/To Do" tab. 

<img src="/images/E%20Learning/SLS_Home.jpg" style="width:75%"/>

**D) <u>COMPLETING YOUR HOMEWORK</u>**
1. Click on “OPEN” to begin SLS homework
2. The “To Do” tab in this picture shows there is 1 activity that is incomplete. All activities have to be completed.
