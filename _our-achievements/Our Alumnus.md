<p>🌟 <strong>CELEBRATING OUR ALUMNUS</strong> 🌟</p>
<p>We are incredibly proud of Harideertham Sri Vishodhan, our former student,
who has achieved stellar results in the 2025 GCE 'O' Level Examination
with 8 distinctions!</p>
<p>Since his early days at AMKP, Harideertham has consistently demonstrated
the values we hold dear – resilience, service and excellence. His achievements
extend beyond academics:</p>
<p>He represented AMKP in the National Primary School Track and Field Championship.</p>
<p>He earned a Certificate of Accomplishment (2023) and Certificate of Distinction
(2025) at the Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation</p>
<p>He represented AMKP and his secondary school in several Mathematics Olympiad
i.e. Raffles Institution Primary Mathematics World Contest in 2019, Singapore
Mathematics Olympiad and Singapore Junior Chemistry Olympiad in 2024</p>
<p>As he embarks on the next chapter of his educational journey, we know
Harideertham will continue to shine and make a positive impact wherever
he goes, exemplifying what it means to be a AMKP graduate - a leader with
character and intellect. &nbsp;</p>
