>SCOUTS

**<font size=5>About</font>**

In Ang Mo Kio Primary School, every AMKPian is welcomed to join Scouts. Scouting focuses on the holistic development of youth and encourages putting others before self. It is also a platform to inculcate discipline, leadership qualities and teamwork through our comprehensive training programmes, excursions, competitions and camps.

  

Scouting provides opportunities for them to solve problems through resilience, collaboration and creativity. AMKP Cub Scouts will also be given the platforms to learn basic Scouting skills such as knots tying, cooking, sewing and pitching of tents during meetings and camps.

  

**The Scout Promise**  
On my Honour I promise that I will do my best  
To do my duty to God and the Republic of Singapore  
To help other people and  
To keep the Scout Law

  
**The Scout Law**  
A Scout is to be trusted  
A Scout is loyal  
A Scout makes friends, establishes and maintains harmonious relations  
A Scout is disciplined and considerate  
A Scout has courage in all difficulties
