<p><strong>About</strong>
</p>
<p>The Choir CCA is an exciting new co-curricular activity (CCA) offered
at AMK primary school, aimed at nurturing students' musical talents and
fostering a love for singing. Through the power of voice, this CCA is designed
to instil discipline, teamwork, and a deep appreciation for music in our
young learners.</p>
