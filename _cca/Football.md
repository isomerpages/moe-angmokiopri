&gt;**Football**

**<font size="5">About</font>**

The AMKP Football CCA introduces a series of ball and game management skills during trainings. Values such as responsibility, resilience, excellent and teamwork are the core to our team ethos.&nbsp;Football comes with a globally large following hence our CCA team is able to harness social unity and promotes friendship. As a sport, it strengthens team spirit and builds self-confidence. Through the acquisition of footballing skills, a player develops his agility, motor skills and coordination as well as spatial awareness.
