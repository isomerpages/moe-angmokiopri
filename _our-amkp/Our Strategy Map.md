<p>Our Strategy Map shows how we work together as a school to support our
vision of "<strong>Every Child a Confident Person, a Joyful Learner and a Caring Citizen</strong>".</p>
<p></p>
<p>We aim to make AMKP a <strong>second home for our students</strong>, where
they feel safe, valued and encouraged to do their best and realise their
potential.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Our work is guided by three key ideas: <strong>Transform, Calibrate and Enculturate</strong>.
This means continually improving learning experiences for students, ensuring
our systems support meaningful work and well-being, and building a positive
and caring school culture.</p>
<p></p>
<p>These priorities are carried out through our <strong>five Strategic Thrusts</strong>,
which guide our efforts in teaching and learning, student development,
staff growth, partnerships, and school operations. Together, they help
us create a supportive environment where every child can truly <strong>Rise and Shine</strong>.</p>
