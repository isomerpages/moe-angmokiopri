<p><strong>About</strong>
</p>
<p>The Brass Band CCA aims to groom students to become confident individuals
by providing them a platform to express themselves musically. Learning
different genres of music has helped our students acquire the skill of
reading musical notes. With a positive attitude and confidence in themselves
as well as the team, our students work together to perform each musical
piece beautifully and confidently.</p>
