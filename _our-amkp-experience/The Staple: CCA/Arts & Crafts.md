<p><strong>About</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The Arts &amp; Crafts Club provides opportunities for our students to
enrich their learning in the aesthetic domain. Our students will learn
the importance of imagination and expression as they are exposed to a wide

and encourage them to pursue their passion in the aesthetics.</p>
