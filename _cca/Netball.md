>NETBALL

**<font size=5>About</font>**

Our Netball CCA introduces a series of ball and game skills during trainings. The teachers, coach and CCA members strongly believe that Netball develops deeper values of responsibility, resilience, teamwork and a desire to succeed through training and competitions.  

Since AMKP started the Netball CCA in 2007, the team has been growing steadily in numbers. Our team of dedicated coach and CCA teachers take pride in leading the CCA. Together, the team strives towards achieving our fullest potential in the sport and in character.
