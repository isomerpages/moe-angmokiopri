<p><strong>About</strong>
</p>
<p>The Track and Field CCA aims to develop students who are enthusiastic
and passionate about athletics. With every training session focusing on
acquiring the core skills of running, jumping and throwing, our students
develop their resilience as they go through the rigorous endurance training
sessions.</p>
