<p><strong>About</strong>
</p>
<p>The Badminton CCA aims to bring joy to our students as they learn to play
the game for recreation or as a competitive sport. Through acquiring the
fundamental skills of using different strokes, proper serving form and
footwork, our students will be able to develop confidence in themselves
as they progress from training to competition mode.</p>
