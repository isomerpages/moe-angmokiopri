&gt;MODERN DANCE

**<font size="5">About</font>**

The dance ensemble aims to develop a resilient and enthusiastic dancer with the willingness to develop and enhance their dance skills through the CCA. Dance ensemble is a competitive CCA which comprises of a dynamic and hardworking team members. The dancers build up on developing their strength and stamina during the practice sessions. They motivate each other in giving their fullest efforts when engaging in preparations for dance competitions. We have a group of passionate dancers who strive to give their best and have the drive to keep their passion in dancing constantly alive.
