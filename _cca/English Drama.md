&gt;**English Drama**

**<font size="5">About</font>**

The vision for our English Drama is that&nbsp;every English Drama member will become a confident and self-expressive individual.

This year, English Drama Club members will learn to appreciate a variety of theatre performances of different styles (e.g. Puppetry), as well as have opportunities to express their creativity and develop their presentation skills.&nbsp;English Drama will also be a platform for students to nurture values such as respect, resilience and care as they learn skills in critical and inventive thinking and self and social awareness.
